Hi friends!

I am incredibly flattered that you’ve visited my site, and even more touched that you want to learn more about me. Here are some quick facts about myself:

I love cats. I’m that person that will seek out the cat at a party and spend the rest of the evening convincing it to love me back. I have two kitties of my own, the 20lb bundle of love Pumpkin, and the crazy kitten Tommy.

I’m currently majoring in Women’s Studies at the University of Calgary cause I wanna teach sexual education.

Cheesy popcorn gives me life. As does white wine, donairs, and overnight oats.

I love coffee, tea, and everything in between. I will never judge you for your coffee shop selection if you let me join you for a date.

I’m obsessed with tattoos. All styles, all types; I want them all.

Kelowna, Scotland, and London are my very favorite places in the world. I will never get over how much I love BC’s gorgeous waters or Scotland’s moody weather. It is all perfect to me.

I married my husband Tim in July 2016. We filled our day with all our friends, dance parties, and breakfast for dinner.
