WEDDINGS

I’ve been doing photography since I was 17, but my wedding day was a game-changer for my work. I spent so much time planning and comparing my wedding to other peoples. I struggled with the fact that we invited 220 people, and not a “small intimate gathering” that other people insisted on. I struggled that I didn’t have a models body in my dress and that I stained the bottom purple just because I felt like it. We didn’t have an outdoor sunset lit venue like Pinterest recommended for me.

We did, however, have the best day we’ve ever had, simply because we made it. I want you to know that your day will be perfect no matter what décor, venue, or catering you have. You are making this day to celebrate your love and that is so, so precious.

I promise to treat your wedding like it’s the best thing in the world because you know what? It is. Your day is incredible because it is all about you and your partner and your loved ones (whether that’s 20 or 200). I want to capture you as a couple and everything that makes you guys love each other even more.

You deserve to have your day captured beautifully, I want to do that for you.

Eloping? Having a cocktail hour ceremony? Shoot me an e-mail and we can figure out how to best meet your needs!
