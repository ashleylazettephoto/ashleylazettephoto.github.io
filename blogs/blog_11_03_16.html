GRACE AND DOUG

Grace & Doug’s wedding was perfect. Like, dancing till 3am, bridal party flash mob, gorgeous sunny day-kind of perfect. And before that was the stunning Nigerian ceremony, full of colour & laughter. I don’t think I’ve ever been so excited for an event! So when I ended up photographing the traditional Nigerian ceremony and some of the reception, I was absolutely honoured by the opportunity. To capture such an honest, genuine love isn’t something one should take lightly.

I have known Grace for around 10 years, but I have only really known her for the past few. Grace is wise. She’s funny and witty and clever. She’s also passionate and firm in what she believes. And I promise that her words are selected with such care that when she compliments you, she means it. Doug is so kind-hearted. He listens with empathy and isn’t judgemental about anything that you’ll say. He always goes about things with humility and rarely speaks without thinking. The two of them together are so, so great. I don’t know what’s in store for them but I do know that it’ll be full to the brim with excellence.

So here are some of the many shots from the Ajele-Campbell wedding, this day will always hold a special place in my heart.
