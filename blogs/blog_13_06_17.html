ALEX + RENEE

I feel incredibly blessed to have met Alex. We worked together at Starbucks, and any day with her was so much fun. She is funny, kind, and everyone who meets her would describe her as the sweetest girl in the world. So of course I said yes when she asked me to photograph her and her twin sister Renee for grad!

Alex and her boyfriend Daniel are such a lovely match. They just mesh so well, and honestly it was pretty effortless to photograph them. Their look also gave off some major “Mr. and Mrs. Smith” vibes which was amazing to work with.

There aren’t many photos of Renee and Geoff that don’t feature them mid-laugh, but I’d say that’s an accurate description of them as a couple; always laughing together. And let’s be honest: having a couple that’s always having a good time together is never a problem for a photographer. We settled on shooting in the Badlands of Alberta, which is now one of my top five places to shoot!

Venue: Drumheller, Alberta

Make-up & Hair: Adrianna Fuller

Dresses: David’s Bridal

When I asked Renee to describe Alex, she said, ”Alex is my best friend, she's my true soulmate. She's someone that has my back no matter what, that kind of person in your life who just happens to know how I feel or what I'm thinking without me even telling her. I'd honestly be so broken without her, she's my other half. She's creative, smart, motivated, so funny, and I'm so proud of her. I love her forever.”

When I asked Alex to describe Renee, she said “She has this beautiful giggle that she does whenever she thinks something is funny, which is pretty much always. Renee is considerate and compassionate and always cares about other people, and looks for ways she can brighten people’s lives. She’s a role model to me in the way that she doesn’t let people get to her. She’s strong and she’s fierce. I love her so much!”

xoxo Ashley
