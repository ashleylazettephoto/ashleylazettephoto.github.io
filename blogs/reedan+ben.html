REEDAN + BEN

Welcome Reedan and Ben, the cutest couple that happened to answer my Instagram model call. If you’ve been following me for a while, you might recognize Reedan from my “Mini but Mighty” post. Reedan and Ben are the parents of sweet little Forrester. But on this summer evening, it was just these two. Just Reedan and Ben, happily walking through the creek and whispering inappropriate jokes into each other’s ears. Near the end of their session, Reedan shared with me that they were expecting (which was one of the best surprises to hear during a session) and asked if we could take some photos of their future babe.

Now that Reedan and Ben have announced their pregnancy, I’m excited to include those “secret” photos in this post.

Congratulations to this growing family, I’m thankful that I got to capture your love on that summer night.
