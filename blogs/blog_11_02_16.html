CAILYN

It took me so long to write this post, mainly because I don’t know what to say. I love Cailyn very much. We met a few years ago through our mutual friend Margaret, and somehow I ended up becoming best friends with this compassionate, honest, genuine individual. It’s not my place to tell you the struggles that Cailyn has been through, but I will tell you that I’m so encouraged by the way that she has carried herself through them.

It was amazing photographing Cailyn, and I want you all to know that this girl isn’t the same girl that I first became friends with. When Cailyn and I went to Europe together a few years ago, she didn’t want me to take any photos of her. I don’t know if it’s because we were sleep deprived and sweaty in 30 degree weather or because she didn’t want to see herself in any of the photos that I took, but what I do know is that she has changed. She was so natural infront of my camera! She just opened herself up and was willing to let me take a million shots of her. I’m just so proud of who she’s become since I first met her, and I know that everyone else in her life is proud of her too.

So here are just some of the many gorgeous shots that we captured together. I hope you love them as much as I love you Cailyn, and that you realize just how incredibly beautiful you (and your million tattoos) are.

xoxo Ashley
