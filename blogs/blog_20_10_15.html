Welcome to Ashley Lazette Photography!

I am so excited to finally launch a photography blog to showcase my work!

A little about me:

My name is Ashley Lazette, and I am a Women’s Studies student from Calgary, Alberta. I love photography, calligraphy, and cats. Trust me, I love cats. I have two: Pumpkin (12) and Thomas Olliver (4 months), and they are basically the sweetest cats you’ll ever meet. When I’m not taking photos or cuddling kitties, I’m a full-time barista and tea-addict. I’m also engaged to Tim, a dog-loving Engineering student (I guess opposites DO attract!).

I took an interest in photography as soon as I got my first point-and-shoot, and soon upgraded to DSLR for my first wedding at 16. It was scary, but easily one of the best opportunities I have ever had! I decided to put my passion on hold while I pursued schooling, which I have no regrets about since Women’s Studies is the best possible degree for me! I love everything I learn, and the environment I’m surrounded by is incredibly encouraging. I have recently taken a temporary leave of absence, which has allowed me to take my Photography Blog into full swing!

If you have anymore questions about me, please don’t hesitate to ask! I would love to grab a coffee and chat about your photography needs.

xoxo Ashley
