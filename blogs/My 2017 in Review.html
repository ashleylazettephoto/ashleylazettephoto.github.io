MY 2017 IN REVIEW

2017 was a wild year for me. Tim and I moved to Edmonton, which meant that all of my contacts were now 3 hours away. I thought that my business would suffer and that my dream of being a wedding photographer would die.

Turns out that this year would be the best year for my business so far. I was given so many opportunities to work with other photographers and I met so many incredible people. I also stopped my business-related bad habits and made sure that my contracts and business was all legally in-check.

Most importantly I was trusted by so many of you amazing people. You trusted me with your memories, your wedding days, and your friendships. You let me into your homes and trusted that I would capture the best part of you. Thank you for trusting me. I am so, so lucky.

xoxo Ashley

Ashley Lazette Photography | Calgary, AB
