BECCA & SARAH

I met Becca through Instagram, which is apparently my favorite way to get to know other photographers haha. Her love for creamy skin tones and cozy sessions drew me to her work, so when she answered a model call that I had I was STOKED!

Becca and Sarah’s love story is the stuff you see in movies. Sarah proposed as Becca was walking out of her gate at the airport, the same way she asked to be her girlfriend. Sarah is probably the most romantic person I’ve ever met.

In-home sessions are my absolute favorite. They are so intimate, romantic, and cozy. People can relax in their own home, and it’s a lovely alternative to an outdoor session when the weather is too cold for comfort.

Thanks for letting me hang out in your home ladies, I had an awesome time hanging out, cuddling your cute dog and being fed tasty omelettes.

xoxo Ashley
