CAILYN

Photographing Cailyn is always an awesome time. Being one of my closest friends, we work so well together and we feel comfortable with each other. So when she hired me to take photos in one of Phil & Seb’s cafes, I was obviously incredibly excited! We rented the space and decided to pursue a black & white portrait session (something I’ve always wanted to do, but have felt nervous about). Photographers are often discouraged from posting B&W images, hugely because they don’t get as many likes/clicks as colored images. But there is something so elegant about black and white. It sophisticates your subject while also capturing emotion in a way that color doesn’t. Now while I love keeping the natural tones and colors of a moment, I am so happy with the way this shoot turned out. Thank you, Cailyn, for trusting me once again, and for allowing me to pursue new projects!

xoxo Ashley
