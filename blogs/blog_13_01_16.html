MARGARET

3, 024 miles. That’s how far Margaret is while she attends university in Antigonish. Now if you ask me, that’s 3,024 miles too many, but I know that she is right where she needs to be.

I’ve actually known Margaret since we were both in elementary school (please don’t ever look up those photos) but we reconnected later in her highschool years when we were a little more mature and much better looking. Since then it’s been all breakfast dates, constant laughter, and confessions of our deepest struggles. Margaret is 3,024 miles away from me right now, but I don’t feel it like I should. When I need her, she’s there for me and I know that I can ask her for help with anything. I mean sure, it’s nice when we get to actually go to breakfast together, but I appreciate her texts just as much.

That’s the kind of person that Margaret is; present in your life without actually needing to be present physically. I’m just one of the many people who get to call her friend and I am so thankful that I do! She’s funny, light-hearted, kind, and sends only the best of vibes to everyone she meets. I can’t wait to have her stand with me as a bridesmaid when I get married.

Love you endlessly, babe.

xoxo Ashley
